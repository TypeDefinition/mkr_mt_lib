13  * Threadsafe stack.
14  *
15  * Invariants:
16  * - top_ == nullptr means that the stack is empty.
17  * - Traversing top_->next_ will eventually lead to the bottom node.
18  *
19  * Addtional Requirements:
20  * - threadsafe_stack must be able to support type T where T is a non-copyable or non-movable type.
21  * - threadsafe_stack does not have to support a non-copyable AND non-movable type.
22  *
23  * Additional Notes:
24  * - threadsafe_stack is non-copyable AND non-movable. (As it has non-copyable and non-movable members.)
